Building a Tutoring Business:

Our recent new business building work

Working side-by-side with our clients to build new businesses â€’ whether starting from scratch or expanding into new areas.
Problem

Despite being a highly sought-after freelance tutor renowned for expertise in a specific region, our client faced challenges in scaling their tutoring services into a full-fledged business. Administrative tasks like SCHEDULING, PAYMENT COLLECTION, and reaching a broader audience beyond local networks became overwhelming. The absence of a digital presence limited their ability to attract new students and expand their tutoring business.

Solution

Drawing on our expertise, we provided a tailored solution to transform our client's freelance tutoring gig into a thriving business. We developed a bespoke WEBSITE and MOBILE APPLICATION, allowing students to easily access course information, schedule classes, and make online payments. With user-friendly features like interactive lesson plans and progress tracking, the platform facilitated seamless communication between our client and their students. Additionally, we implemented automated booking systems to streamline administrative tasks and minimize workload. To enhance our client's online visibility, we employed targeted DIGITAL MARKETING strategies, including SEARCH ENGINE OPTIMIZATION (SEO) and SOCIAL MEDIA ADVERTISING, to attract new students from the target region.

Result

With our comprehensive solution in place, our client successfully transitioned from freelance tutor to established business owner. The user-friendly platform improved student satisfaction and retention rates, while automated booking systems reduced administrative burden. Our DIGITAL MARKETING efforts significantly expanded our client's online reach, resulting in a surge of new student registrations and inquiries. Within a short timeframe, our client's tutoring business experienced exponential growth, establishing them as a prominent player in the education industry. Overall, our expertise empowered our client to scale their tutoring services effectively and achieve remarkable success in their business endeavors.
